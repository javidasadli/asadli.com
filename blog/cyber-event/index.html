My journay during Black Hat conference 10 October 2016

Oxford Training Sessions on the Modern Information Society Session No.3: “Cyberspace and Society: New Threats and Opportunities for Welfare” event was held at TallTech during September 30 - October 2 2016. The Oxford Training Sessions discuss innovative uses of information technology in Estonia and elsewhere to identify, clarify, and model how changing configurations in cyberspace influence the structures and processes of political systems within and across national borders.

3-day event was very useful for in my first semester at TallTech. Speakers gave very brief information about the cyber security. Additionally, we had a simulation exercise at the 3rd day of event which were held in formulating groups and provided the outcomes such as critical computer systems, awareness, information society and etc.
